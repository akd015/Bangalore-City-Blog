Top Attractions

Green parks, regal palaces, living history, and weekend escapes.

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

18th-century garden with the iconic Glass House and rare flora.

Commissioned by Hyder Ali and expanded by Tipu Sultan, Lalbagh is a horticultural treasure with serene lakes and heritage trees.

Cubbon Park

Lush lawns, avenues, and institutions in the city’s heart.

Home to the State Central Library and near the Visvesvaraya Museum — Cubbon Park is where the city comes to breathe.

Vidhana Soudha

Majestic seat of Karnataka’s legislature.

A Neo-Dravidian architectural landmark symbolizing Karnataka’s governance and pride.

Bengaluru Palace

Tudor-style royal residence with ornate interiors.

Built by the Wodeyars, the palace reflects colonial-era grandeur and hosts cultural events.

Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace

Teakwood masterpiece from the 18th century.

A glimpse into Mysorean architecture and Tipu Sultan’s legacy, with frescoes and ornate pillars.

ISKCON Temple Bengaluru

Grand hilltop temple complex in Rajajinagar.

One of the largest ISKCON temples in the world, drawing devotees and tourists alike.

Visvesvaraya Industrial & Technological Museum

Interactive science museum for all ages.

Hands-on exhibits celebrating engineering and innovation, named after Sir M. Visvesvaraya.

HAL Heritage Centre & Aerospace Museum

Aircraft, engines, and India’s aviation story.

Showcases HAL’s milestones with outdoor aircraft displays and flight simulators.

Bannerghatta Biological Park

Safari, butterfly park, and a zoo amid forested hills.

A popular day trip for wildlife lovers south of the city.

Nandi Hills (Weekend Escape)

A sunrise favorite about 60–70 km from the city.

Hill fortress with viewpoints, Tipu’s Drop, and pleasant weather — perfect for early risers.

UB City & Vittal Mallya Road

Luxury mall, restaurants, and nightlife.

A chic retail and dining destination near MG Road and Cubbon Park.

KR Market & Commercial Street

Bargains, colors, and local life.

From flowers at KR Market to apparel and accessories at Commercial Street — shopping spans traditional to trendy.
