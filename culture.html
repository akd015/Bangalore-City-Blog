Culture, Food & Events

Traditions, tech, and tastes that define Bengaluru.

Must-Try Foods

From tiffin classics to hearty staples.

  • Masala Dosa & Benne Dosa
  • Bisi Bele Bath
  • Ragi Mudde with Sambar
  • Mangaluru Buns, Idli-Vada
  • Filter Coffee & Craft Beer (pub culture)

Festivals & Traditions

Community and color through the year.

  • Bengaluru Karaga — ancient temple procession in April.
  • Dasara/Dussehra — city celebrations; Mysuru nearby hosts the grand procession.
  • Lalbagh Flower Shows — Jan & Aug around national days.
  • Kadlekai Parishe — groundnut fair on Bull Temple Road.
  • Bengaluru International Film Festival (BIFFES) — cinema from India and the world.

Tech & Startup Culture

India’s innovation capital.

Bengaluru hosts global tech giants and thousands of startups. Co-working spaces, meetups, and hackathons fuel a collaborative ecosystem.

Music, Arts & Nightlife

Gigs, galleries, and theatres.

From classical Carnatic recitals to indie rock and electronic, the city’s stages are busy. Galleries, comedy clubs, and microbreweries round out the scene.
