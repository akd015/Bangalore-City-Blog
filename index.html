Welcome to Bangalore (Bengaluru)

Founded in 1537 by Kempe Gowda I, Bengaluru sits on the Deccan Plateau at ~900 m elevation — giving it a mild, pleasant climate most of the year. It’s known for tree-lined avenues, thriving startups, and a vibrant food and music scene.

Nicknames include the Garden City of India and the Silicon Valley of India. The city is served by Kempegowda International Airport, BMTC buses, and the ever-expanding Namma Metro.

Quick Facts

  • State: Karnataka
  • Elevation: ~900 m (highest among major Indian cities)
  • Climate: Tropical wet & dry; pleasant year-round
  • Languages: Kannada (official), English, Hindi, others
  • Famous for: IT industry, gardens, cafes, craft beer
  • Best time: Oct–Feb
